About This Survey

Purpose

This survey is designed to assess the needs, experiences, and challenges faced by LGBTQI+ individuals in our community. The data collected will help local council members and community organizations better understand how to support and serve our LGBTQI+ residents.

Privacy & Confidentiality

All survey responses are completely anonymous. No personally identifiable information is collected. Data is stored securely and used only for community planning and resource allocation purposes.

Local Support Services

  • Crisis Support: Call 988 for immediate help
  • Mental Health: Community counseling services available
  • Support Groups: Weekly meetups for peer support
  • Educational Programs: Workshops on rights and resources

Know Your Rights

  • Employment: Protection from workplace discrimination
  • Housing: Fair housing laws protect LGBTQI+ individuals
  • Healthcare: Right to respectful, affirming medical care
  • Education: Safe, inclusive learning environments

Understanding LGBTQI+ Terms

L
Lesbian - Women who are emotionally and/or sexually attracted to other women
G
Gay - Men who are emotionally and/or sexually attracted to other men
B
Bisexual - People attracted to more than one gender
T
Transgender - People whose gender identity differs from their assigned sex at birth
Q
Queer/Questioning - Umbrella term or people exploring their identity
I
Intersex - People born with physical sex characteristics that don't fit typical definitions
+
Plus - Represents other identities and the diversity of human experience
